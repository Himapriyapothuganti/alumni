Get Involved

The VNRVJIET Alumni Association aims to maintain an engaging platform connecting a community of alumni and presently enrolled students at the College.

We facilitate meaningful connections between current students and alumni, fostering mentorship opportunities and career guidance. Whether seeking professional advice or exploring potential career paths, we serve as a bridge between generations, empowering individuals to navigate their academic and professional journeys with confidence.

Student or not, VNRVJIET will always be here for you. This is your community.
